Welcome to the Northstar Ski Resort Page

Northstar California Resort is a popular ski resort located in the Lake Tahoe area of California. The resort offers over 3,000 acres of skiable terrain, ranging from beginner to expert levels, with 100 trails and 20 lifts. In addition to skiing and snowboarding, Northstar also offers other winter activities such as snowshoeing, cross-country skiing, and ice-skating. During the summer months, the resort transforms into a mountain biking and hiking destination, with scenic trails and stunning mountain views. Northstar is known for its luxurious accommodations, excellent dining options, and family-friendly atmosphere, making it a top destination for both winter and summer vacations.
